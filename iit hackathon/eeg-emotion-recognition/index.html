About BIMBO AI

An intelligent EEG-based emotion recognition platform that combines cutting-edge signal processing, advanced machine learning algorithms, and interactive visualization to decode human emotions from brain activity patterns. The system employs scientifically validated preprocessing techniques, multi-band frequency analysis, and state-of-the-art classification models to achieve industry-leading accuracy in affective computing.

Model Accuracy: 80-90% with XGBoost | Upload Limit: 1 GB
